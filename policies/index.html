Privacy/Cookie policy

I do not directly use any cookies whatsoever, be it for tracking, functional purposes, or otherwise.

Any cookies set or fetched on this website belong to 3rd party sources.

For example, some project captions use shields.io images and, like most Content Delivery Networks, shields.io use a DDOS protection service like Cloud Flare.

Cloud Flare sets a cookie called "__cfduid" which is used for tracking.

I cannot control this.

To opt out from this, I recommend blocking all cookies on the jonmarsh.dev domain.

As there are many web browsers out there, I cannot possibly give instructions for all of them.

So, please refer to the manual/help section of your web browser or simply search online for how to disable cookies.

Thank you.
